Performs "pagination" of specified IQueryable<T> source, returning a fragment ("page") of its contents.

Template Parameters
TType of elements in IQueryable<T> source.
Parameters
selfSource from which a fragment is to be taken.
pageNumber of fragment/slice that is to be taken. Numbering starts from 1.
pageSizeSize of fragment ("page"), number of entities to be taken. Must be a positive number.
Returns
Source that represent a fragment of the original self and consists of no more than pageSize elements.
